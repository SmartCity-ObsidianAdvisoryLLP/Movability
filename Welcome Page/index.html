What are we Solving?

We aim to simplify and optimise the cumbersome and inefficient REHAB-BUS transport system in Hong Kong. We use data analytics for optimal allocation of resources for the disabled. We also provide a one stop solution to booking rehab buses and knowing the status of the bookings as well as bus timings.

Current Scenario

Our Products

We have two products to offer. One catering to the needs of the consumers (riders) and the other for the company offering rehab bus services.

Mobile Application for Riders

A mobile application allowing the users to view the rehab bus timings and booking them.

Dashboard

A dashboard rendering routes information, data from the mobile application in a visualised form. It also provides the optimal routes for buses based on different kinds of disability. Helping the company to keep a track of the system.

App Solution Workflow

Search

Search for the buses available on your desired route and time.

Request

Request a bus at your desired pick up point on a particular date and time.

Track

Track the status of your booking and ride.
